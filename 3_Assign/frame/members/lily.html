Lily Jin Morrow

(릴리 머로우; born Oct 17, 2002), simply known as Lily,
is a Korean-Australian actress and singer. She is a member of the Kpop group NMIXX under JYP Entertainment.
She was a contestant in the reality show Kpop Star 4. Lily made her debut with NMIXX on February 22, 2022 at the age of 19.
